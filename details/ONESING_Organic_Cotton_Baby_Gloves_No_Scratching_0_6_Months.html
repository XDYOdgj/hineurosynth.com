ONESING Organic Cotton Baby Gloves

Designed for babies 0-6 months, these soft and stretchy gloves are made from organic cotton and crafted to protect your baby's delicate skin from self-scratching. The breathable material ensures comfort during those early days of exploration, while the neutral color palette blends effortlessly with any baby outfit.

These gloves are not only sustainable and eco-friendly but also built to last, making them a reusable addition to your baby's essential items. Perfect for keeping your little one's hands safe and cozy, they’re easy to put on and take off, ideal for busy parents and newborn care routines.

For Babies 6-20 Months

These no-scratch gloves are also ideal for babies 6-20 months, offering extra protection against the cold while allowing for a snug but comfortable fit. The wide elastic band ensures that the gloves stay on without restricting movement, making them perfect for both boys and girls.

With a 9cm length and one-size-fits-all design, they're adaptable to growing hands. Made from high-quality organic cotton, they're gentle on the skin and a thoughtful choice for parents who value sustainability and comfort in every detail.
