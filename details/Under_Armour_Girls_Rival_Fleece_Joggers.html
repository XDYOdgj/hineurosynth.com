Under Armour Girls' Rival Fleece Joggers

Designed for active girls who demand both performance and style. These joggers are perfect for everyday wear, workouts, or casual outings.

The Under Armour Girls' Rival Fleece Joggers combine comfort with cutting-edge performance. Constructed from a mid-weight, breathable, and moisture-wicking fleece, they keep you warm and dry in any weather. The soft interior offers a cozy feel, making them ideal for long days on the move.
