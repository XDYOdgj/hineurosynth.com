SANGTREE Girls Drawstring Cargo Jogger Pants

These stylish and functional jogger pants are perfect for active little girls. Made with a soft blend of 97% cotton and 3% polyester, they offer a comfortable, breathable feel that keeps your child cool and at ease throughout the day. The elastic waist design provides a snug, flexible fit that moves with their body, eliminating any tightness or restriction. With a tapered leg style and secure elastic ankle cuffs, these pants add a modern, flattering look to any outfit.

Why You'll Love These Pants

  • Soft & Durable Fabric: The 97% cotton and 3% polyester blend ensures both comfort and long-lasting wear, ideal for everyday adventures.
